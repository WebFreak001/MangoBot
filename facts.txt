The scientific name of a mango is "Mangifera Indica"
Mangos are grown in the tropics
Mangos typically measure from 5 to 15 centimeters in length and 4 to 10 centimeters in width
Fresh mango season lasts from April until August
The mango flesh is called mesocarp
Mangos are an excellent source of Vitamin-A and flavonoids like beta-carotene, alpha-carotene, and beta-cryptoxanithin
Mango has been found to protect against colon, breast, leukemia and prostate cancers
